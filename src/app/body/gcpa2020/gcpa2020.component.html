GLOBAL CHILD PRODIGY AWARDS 2020

The Global Child Prodigy Awards 2020 were presented by Nobel Peace Laureate Shri Kailash Satyarthi and Dr. Kiran Bedi, Honorable Lt. Governor of Puducherry. The recognition that was sprawled across thirty different categories included dance, palaeontology, biking, choreography, fitness, martial arts, art, mathematicians, science, singing, music, enterprise, and many more. It is the worlds first initiative to honour 100 children from around 45 countries around the globe, which was concluded at the Asoka Hotel in New Delhi, India on January 3rd, 2020. The ceremony was graced by eminent personalities like Noble Laureate Kailash Satyarthi, Shri Achyuta Samantha, MP-Lok Sabha and Founder of KIIT and KISS, Shri T H Vinayakaram, Grammy Awardee, Prof Anil D Sahasrabudhe, Chairman-AICTE, and Dr.Kiran Bedi, Honorable Lt. Governor of Puducherry. The guest list further includes some Padma Awardees, former ministers, and members of parliament.

About GCP Awards:

GCP Awards is the first platform of its kind with the objective of recognizing child prodigies in different categories such as arts, music, dance, writing, modeling, acting, science, sports and many others. This initiative aims at giving young talents the global exposure that they deserve and provide them with the right opportunities at the right time to ensure that they create a major impact on society.
