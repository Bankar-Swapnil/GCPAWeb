GLOBAL CHILD PRODIGY AWARDS 2022

A special ceremony honoured the 100 top global child prodigies for their achievements. Under The Patronage of His Highness Sheikh Nahayan Mabarak AI Nahayan, Cabinet Member and Minister of Tolerance & Coexistence, Government of the UAE, the Global Child Prodigy Awards 2022 was held on 20th August 2022, at Marriott Al Jaddaf, Dubai. Nobel Prize Winner Sir Richard J. Roberts, Dr Azad Moopen, Chairman and managing director - Aster DM Healthcare were the guests of honour for the award ceremony. H.H Sheikh Nahyan officially launched the book 'Top 100 Child Prodigies 2022-2023' featuring the Top 100 Child Prodigies. It will be distributed to top libraries around the world. The top 100 child prodigies hail from diverse fields, including martial arts, painting, modelling, entrepreneurship, writing, social work, acting, and many more. The Awards were presented by His Highness Sheikh Nahayan Mabarak, Dr Azad Moopen and Nobel Prize winner Sir Richard J. Roberts.

About GCP Awards:

GCP Awards is the first platform of its kind with the objective of recognizing child prodigies in different categories such as arts, music, dance, writing, modeling, acting, science, sports and many others. This initiative aims at giving young talents the global exposure that they deserve and provide them with the right opportunities at the right time to ensure that they create a major impact on society.
